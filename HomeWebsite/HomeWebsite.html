HOME

The house:"Venkateshwara Nilayam was built by the shepherd family member Annaiah.N who is from Kamundoddi village, near Hosur. This house was built because of my Mother Bharathi.N, owning a home was one of biggest lifelong dreams and i am extremely glad that the house was built.

Living hall

The grand house warming ceremony was done in the year 2011 amongst the gracious presence of friends and relatives. It was a proud moment for my family.

ABOUT

The house faces towards North and has three floors including the ground floor. Ample parking space to park a car, three motorcycles and two bicycles. The ground floor has three rooms, it is where we stay. The second floor has two hoses with bedrooms in each house. Further, the second floor has two bedrooms with a huge varandah.

FAMILY

My mother is the beacon of the family, she has the most sincere and the best work ethic i have ever seen. She takes care of the house like it is a piece of gold, she always used to keep it crystal clear and clean.

She is the light of the house, my life will never be the same again without her. I have so many memories with her, mother is the only person who shows unconditional love and i have lost it.
